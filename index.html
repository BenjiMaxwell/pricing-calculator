Overview

Maxwell Creative Media specializes in commercial photography for small businesses, including corporate headshots, group shots, lifestyle photography, and video content for websites, social media, and marketing. Our pricing is designed to be flexible, allowing custom quotes tailored to your needs. All services include a non-exclusive commercial usage license for website, social media, and digital marketing.

Note: Prices are for shoots within 20 miles of Sunnyvale, CA. Payment terms: 50% deposit to book, 50% upon delivery. Delivery via online gallery within 7–14 days, with previews in 24–72 hours.

Itemized Pricing

1. Session Fees

Covers setup, shooting time, travel (within 20 miles), and basic creative direction at your location (e.g., job site, office). Required for all shoots.

  • Mini Session (1–2 hours): $250
    Ideal for small shoots (e.g., 1–3 headshots). Up to 100 raw images captured.
  • Half-Day Session (3–4 hours): $400
    Suitable for small teams (5–10 people). Up to 200 raw images.
  • Full-Day Session (6–8 hours): $800
    For larger teams or multiple locations (10–20 people). Up to 400 raw images.
  • Multi-Session (8–12 hours, 1–2 days): $1,200
    For premium projects with complex needs. Up to 600 raw images.

Individual professional portraits, shot on-location with basic retouching (color correction, minor blemishes).

How to Craft a Custom Quote

  1. Choose Session Type: Select Mini, Half-Day, Full-Day, or Multi-Session based on shoot duration and complexity.
  2. Add Deliverables: Pick headshots, group shots, lifestyle images, and/or video per client needs.
  3. Include Add-Ons: Add high-res files, video, or social media packs for specific goals.
  4. Calculate Total: Sum session fee, deliverables, and add-ons. Optional 5–10% discount for bookings over $2,000.
  5. Present Quote: Provide a branded PDF with itemized breakdown, terms, and safety requirements (e.g., PPE for job sites).

Contact us to discuss your project and receive a tailored quote. Ask about crew size, locations, and specific needs (e.g., video, print materials).
